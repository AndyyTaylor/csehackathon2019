    Overview

    This upright fridge from Westinghouse comes with a 2-year warranty and is finished in a classic white. Don't be fooled by its size as the storage solutions take up every inch of the cavity, leaving room to fit large food items with ease. If you're looking for a simple and affordable fridge, then the WRM2400WD is for you. This upright fridge comes pigeon pair matching these two WFM1800WD or WFM1810WC Westinghouse upright freezers.
