Fridges

There is a huge range of great fridges available with next day delivery from Appliances Online.

Whether it be a quick replacement model or a premium, feature-rich refrigerator, there is something to suit your family size and price range.

The style of fridge you choose will depend on your habits and kitchen size. Bottom mounts are typically more popular than traditional top mounts. Bottom mounts are great for convenient access with all of your favourites at eye level, so there is less need to bend. For larger households, French doors and four-doors provide plenty of space and help arrange items in a convenient fashion.

They also come with handy features like ice and water dispensers, wine racks and smart control panels. Plumbed and non-plumbed ice-and-water fridges are available, so make sure you have the proper connection options to accompany your fridge.

With so many different colours you can easily pick a fridge that you like and, more importantly, suits your kitchen style. Colourful appliances are very popular at the moment: black fridges may be taking the lead but red, yellow, blue and green are all close behind. 

For those with old school tastes, there are retro fridges available with vintage designs which make a great impression in colourful homes.

Energy efficiency is quite an important consideration to which you can easily find a model that helps save you the most energy possible all year round. Take a look at the Electrolux and Liebherr refrigeration ranges for the highest energy efficiencies available.
